Batata Harra Recipe

Batata Harra recipe for the lovers of potato. It's delicious, limoney, and so tasty.

Ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes (cubed)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Handful of fresh coriander (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of half a lemon

Steps

  1. Boil or fry the cubed potatoes until golden and crispy.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic and chili.
  3. Add paprika, chili flakes, and salt.
  4. Toss the potatoes into the pan and coat them with the spice mix.
  5. Turn off heat and mix in fresh coriander and lemon juice.
  6. Serve hot and spicy!
