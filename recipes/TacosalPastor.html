Tacos al Pastor Recipe

Home
A taco recipe for the lovers of mexican food. It's smoky, citrusy, and so sweet and.

Ingredients

  • 500g thin-sliced pork shoulder
  • 2 dried guajillo chiles (soaked and seeded)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Pineapple chunks
  • Chopped onion & cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Blend chiles, garlic, pineapple juice, vinegar, cumin, oregano, and salt into a marinade.
  2. Marinate pork slices for at least 4 hours (overnight is best).
  3. Grill or pan-cook pork until slightly charred and cooked through.
  4. Warm corn tortillas on a skillet.
  5. Chop grilled pork and assemble in tortillas with pineapple, onion, and cilantro.
  6. Serve with lime wedges.
